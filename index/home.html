          ~~~ About ~~~

          The Website

          Mae govannen! (Hello!) Do you love The Lord of The Rings? Ready to immerse yourself in Middle Earth?
          If you want to test your knowledge of Tolkien's realm or learn what character you would be on an epic quest to destroy The One Ring, then you have come to the right place!
          QuizRealm is a fanmade website for The Lord of The Rings community. We host personality and trivia quizzes for fans to have fun, procrastinate their studies and share with friends.

          The Creator

          My name is Natalie Casey, I'm a student at Edinburgh Napier and a huge fan of The Lord of The Rings.
          This website was created as part of Web Technologies Coursework for Year 3 Cyber Security & Digital Forensics BEng.
          Although just a start-up, there are much bigger plans for QuizRealm. If this site has enough use and is liked by others, more quizzes will be added for the enjoyment of fans.
          Please engage and share with your friends to help me bring QuizRealm to life!
