          ~~~ Character Information ~~~



          Frodo Baggins


          Frodo Baggins is the main character in The Lord of The Rings. He is charged with the task of destroying The One Ring and carries the heaviest burden of all. His personality traits are bravery, independence, curiosity, secretive, resilient, quiet and adventurous. He likes to be in control and fears losing himself to the darkness. He carries a sword, Sting and had to flee from his home in The Shire when the enemy learned he had The Ring. Frodo is a great result to get as he is considered a hero and made it through a dangerous quest across unknown lands.

          Character Quote: "It is useless to meet revenge with revenge: it will heal nothing."


          Samwise Gamgee


          Samwise Gamgee, commonly called Sam is a loyal friend and goes with Frodo to the very end in the quest to destroy The One Ring. He is known around the Shire as the friendly gardener and likes to go out for a drink with Frodo, Merry and Pippin. His personality traits are wholesome, defender, pure, sensible, skeptical, nosy and courageous. After the brutal journey as part of The Fellowship, Sam settles down with Rosie Cotton and has a few kids. He leads a happy life. Getting Sam as your result highlights a person with true intentions and fierce loyalty to friends.

          Character Quote: “That there's some good in this world, Mr. Frodo... and it's worth fighting for.”


          Meriadoc Brandybuck


          Meriadoc Brandybuck, commonly called Merry, is best friends with Pippin and spends a lot of time with Frodo and Sam too. He unexpectly joins The Fellowship in their quest when bumping into Frodo and Sam when they try to leave The Shire. His personality traits are funloving, happy, unsure of strangers, streetwise, proud, reliable, and determined. During their quest, Merry had to go it alone for a while and was able to prove his bravery by fighting alongside Eowyn for Rohan. Getting Merry as your result is good, it shows you are a balanced person who knows how to have fun while still getting the job done.

          Character Quote: "This, my friends, is a pint.""


          Peregrin Took


          Peregrin Took, commonly called Pippin, is best friends with Merry. He loves to drink, sing and party in the shire and will invite many people to join. He is a less confident character as he is often in trouble for not thinking things through and clumsy moments. His personality traits are caring, dark horse, fun, forgetfulness and silliness. Pippin was a close ally to Gandalf later in the quest as he fought by his side at Minas Tirith. He was scared but faced his fears head on. Although associated with some negatives, getting Pippin as a result is not a bad thing. He always means well and comes through in the end. Expect the unexpected and try not to underestimate yourself if you get this result.

          Character Quote: "You need people of intelligence on this sort of mission... quest... thing."
